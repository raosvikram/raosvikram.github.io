About me

I am a second year PhD student in the Department of Computer Science at Cornell University and am interested in Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing.

Before coming to Cornell, I spent four wonderful years as a Computer Science undergraduate at IIT Madras where I was generously supported by the Aditya Birla Scholarship and OPJEMS. Before that, I spent many carefree years in the beautiful south-Indian city of Hyderabad.

I spent Summer 2014 at Twitter, Summer 2012 at Microsoft Research, Redmond and Summer 2011 at Yahoo, Bangalore.
