About me

I am a second year PhD student in the Department of Computer Science at Cornell University.

I have graduated from Cornell with an MS degree and joined Twitter as a Data Scientist.

Before joining Cornell, I spent four wonderful years as a Computer Science undergraduate at IIT Madras where I was generously supported by the Aditya Birla Scholarship and OPJEMS. Before that, I spent many carefree years in the beautiful south-Indian city of Hyderabad.

I've spent summers at Twitter as a graduate student and at Microsoft Research, Redmond and Yahoo, Bangalore as an undergraduate.
