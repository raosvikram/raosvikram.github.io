About

I am a Software Engineer in the Research and Machine Intelligence team at Google. Before that, I worked on Timeline ranking at Twitter, contributing several key pieces to Twitter’s shift from a reverse-chronological timeline to a relevance-ranked feed.

In a previous life, I worked on applied NLP in the CS PhD program at Cornell , which I discontinued with an MS after two years. Even before, I was a CSE undergraduate at IIT Madras where I was generously supported by the Aditya Birla Scholarship and OPJEMS.

I’ve previously spent summers at Twitter SF, Microsoft Research Redmond, and (the now defunct) Yahoo Bangalore.

As a PhD student, I’ve TA-ed several large classes (most enjoyable being Machine Learning for Data Science) and found the experience very valuable.
