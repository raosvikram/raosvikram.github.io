About me

I am a Data Scientist with Timelines at Twitter - we work on ranking your tweets. Before that, I was working on applied NLP in the CS PhD program at Cornell , which I discontinued after two years. Even before, I was a CS undergraduate at IIT Madras where I was generously supported by the Aditya Birla Scholarship and OPJEMS.

I’ve spent summers at Twitter as a graduate student and at MSR Redmond and Yahoo, Bangalore as an undergraduate.

As a PhD student, I’ve TA-ed several large classes (most enjoyable being Machine Learning for Data Science) and found the experience very valuable.
